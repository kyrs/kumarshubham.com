TIIC IDC-2015

TIIC IDC is an anuual university level competition organized by Texas Instrument to bring young innovator on a common platform. We participated in this competition with our idea of Sign Language Converter(SLC) A small and portable device for speech impaired people, capable of converting their hand gesture directly into speech format in real time. Interesting thing about SLC is that it doesn't put any constrain on user to wear any sensor embeded glove and make a user friendly experience. We reached till semi-finals and were listed among Top 80 in a competition of 2000+ people.
